WHY THE LABEL IS NOT ENOUGH

FDA labeler records identify the U.S. marketing entity that filed the application — not the facility where the drug was manufactured. "Manufactured For" shell arrangements, U.S. HQ conflation, and CMO cascading have made it structurally impossible to trace origin from label alone.

Over 80% of U.S. drug Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients are sourced overseas. The FDA inspected 28% of Chinese pharmaceutical facilities in FY2024. For the remaining 72%, procurement officers are buying drugs from facilities they cannot identify — because the label points to a U.S. subsidiary, not the factory.

iRemedy.ai closes that gap. Every determination. Every NDC. Full evidence trail.
